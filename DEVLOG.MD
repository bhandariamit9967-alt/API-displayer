# Devlog — Debate Partner

## Day 1

Built a VS Code extension called Debate Partner. It lets you pick a topic, choose a side, and debate against AI round by round. It scores your arguments and gives feedback.

Started with Anthropic Claude as the AI, then switched to Google Gemini, then finally switched to Groq with Llama 3.3. Cleaned up the code, removed all comments, and fixed the package.json to remove unused dependencies.

Pushed the project to GitHub.
